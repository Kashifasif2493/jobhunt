Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WorldJobShunt ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the remote jobs, career growth, and international opportunities platform hosted at worldjobshunt.com. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website worldjobshunt.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us optimize your job search and platform experience. This includes:

  • Information Provided Voluntarily: If you contact us via our Contact form, submit inquiries, or interact with our portal, we collect your name, email address, and the specific content of your message.
  • Automated Information: We may collect certain non-personally identifiable information automatically when you visit our website, including your browser type, device information, operating system, pages viewed, IP addresses, cookie data, analytics details, and the time and date of your visit.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve our website, respond to your messages and inquiries, analyze usage trends to enhance user experience, display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense, prevent fraud, and ensure overall platform security.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses advertising cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve personalized ads based on browsing behavior. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the official Google Ads Settings page.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Users may opt out of the use of third-party cookies for personalized advertising by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

5. GDPR & CCPA Privacy Rights

We ensure full compliance with regional data privacy laws. Depending on your location, you have the following explicit rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access digital copies of your personal information held by us.
  • The right to request corrections or updates to any inaccurate information.
  • The right to request the complete deletion of your personal data from our systems.
  • The right to request data portability to move your data to another service.
  • The right to opt-out of specific data collection or processing mechanisms where applicable.

6. Third-Party Links

WorldJobShunt links to third-party job listings, external employers, and corporate career portals. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, tracking methods, or content of those external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safety protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is a remote jobs and career portal that is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

10. Contact Us
